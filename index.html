Our story

A hobby that became something we couldn't stop sharing

What started on the couch became Our Daily Thread

It started as something to do with my hands in the evenings — a needle, some thread, and a piece of fabric. What I didn't expect was how much joy it would bring to other people when I started sharing what I was making.

Our Daily Thread is a small family operation. Every single piece is made by hand, made to order, made with care. We don't rush, we don't mass-produce — we stitch each one like it's going to someone we love. Because it usually is.

Thank you for being here and for supporting something made with genuine love.
